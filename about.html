About Green Earth Initiative

Joined in 2018, Green Earth initiative emerged from a small group of environmental advocates who were passionate about creating tangible change in their community. What started as weekend beach cleanups has grown into a comprehensive environmental organization with >500 active volunteers.

Our mission is simple: to create a sustainable future through direct action, education, and community engagement. We believe that environmental change starts at the grassroots level, with individuals and communities working together to protect our planet.

Our Impact

Accomplishments:

  • Removed over 2,000 pounds of plastic waste from local beaches and waterways
  • Educated 300+ students through our school environmental programs
  • Partnered with 15 local businesses to reduce single-use plastics
  • Planted 100 native trees in restored habitat areas
  • Organized 10+ community cleanup events

Our Values

We are committed to transparency, community involvement, and evidence-based solutions. Every action we take is guided by scientific research and the voices of the communities we serve.

Get Involved

Ready to make a difference? There are many ways to join our mission:

  • Volunteer: Join our weekend cleanup events and educational workshops. Drop your contact details
  • Donate: Support our programs with financial contributions
  • Advocate: Help us lobby for environmental policy changes
  • Educate: Share our mission with friends, family, and colleagues
